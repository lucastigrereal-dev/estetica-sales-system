## 2. 3 PHASES

**Phase 1: TESTING (Week 1-2, $5-10/day)**
- Create 5 creatives (different messages)
- Test 2-3 audiences
- Monitor: CTR, CPC, cost per lead
- Goal: Find winners (top 2)

**Phase 2: SCALING (Week 3-4, $10-20/day)**
- Kill losers (pause <1% CTR)
- Double budget on top 2
- Add 1-2 new creatives
- Expand to lookalike 5%
- Goal: 3-4:1 ROAS

**Phase 3: GROWTH (Week 5+, $50-200+/day)**
- Scale winners 50% weekly
- Add retargeting (warm)
- Expand audience gradually
- Add sales ads
- Goal: 3-5:1 ROAS at scale

---

## 3. DAILY METRICS

**Check daily:**

- CTR: >1-2% good, <0.5% bad
- CPC: <$0.50 good, >$1 bad
- CPL: <$1 good, >$3 bad
- ROAS: >3:1 profitable, <2:1 bad

## 5. SCALING WINNERS

**When ad performs (ROAS >3:1):**

- Week 1: $2/day
- Week 2: $4/day (double)
- Week 3: $8/day (double)
- Week 4: $16/day (double)
- Week 5+: 50% weekly increase

**Key:** Gradual scaling prevents cost increase

---

## 6. CREATIVE FATIGUE

**Signs:**
- CTR declining
- CPC increasing
- Same metrics, fewer results

**Solutions:**
- Rotate new creative every 2-4 weeks
- Keep 20% testing budget always
- Archive (don't delete)
- Refresh top performers

---

## 7. AUDIENCE EXPANSION

**Scaling safely:**

- Week 1-2: Lookalike 1% (most similar)
- Week 3-4: Lookalike 1-5% (if ROAS >3:1)
- Week 5+: Lookalike 1-10% (if stable)

**Key:** Expand gradually, monitor ROAS

## 9. A/B TESTING
