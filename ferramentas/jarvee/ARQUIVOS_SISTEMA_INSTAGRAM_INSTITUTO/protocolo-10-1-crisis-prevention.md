## 3. PREVENTION TACTICS

**For controversial content:**
- Review ALL past posts
- Delete/archive truly offensive
- Cool-off period: write ‚Üí wait 24h ‚Üí post
- Review before posting

**For creator drama:**
- Vet partners carefully

**For personal info:**
- 2FA everywhere
- Strong unique passwords
- Separate work email
- Never share exact address

**For scam allegations:**
- Only recommend products you use
- Disclose affiliate links
- Test before recommending
- Money-back guarantee
- Respond quickly to complaints

**For shadowban:**
- Don't use bots
- Don't buy followers
- Organic growth only
- Use Instagram official tools

**For account hacked:**
- 2FA enabled (most important)
- Unique passwords
- Email backup secure
- Monthly password change

## 5. HACKS VALIDATED

**HACK #1: Audit on day 1** (+100%)
- Before 10k followers
- Delete old controversial content
- Prevents future crises

**HACK #2: Cool-off period** (+80%)
- Write hot take
- Wait 24h
- Re-read next day
- 70% fewer regrettable posts

**HACK #3: 2FA everywhere** (+90%)
- Most hacks = password compromise
- 2FA = essentially impossible to hack
- 2 min setup = protected forever

**HACK #4: Over-deliver** (+95%)
- Under-promise, over-deliver
- Zero scam allegations
- Customer gets MORE than expected

**HACK #5: Transparency** (+80%)
- Always disclose affiliate links
- Always say "sponsored"
- Audience appreciates honesty
