# ⚡ PROTOCOLO OPERACIONAL - 10.2
## CRISIS RESPONSE (Reagindo Quando Acontece)

**Métrica Principal:** **Response time < 4 hours**

---

## 1. OBJETIVO

Responder RÁPIDO e CERTO quando crise acontece — minimizar danos.

---

## 2. CRISIS RESPONSE FRAMEWORK

### Step 1: PAUSE (5 min)
- Take 15 min to calm down
- Talk to 1-2 trusted people
- Understand what's happening
- Make rational decision

### Step 2: ASSESS (15 min)
- Is this actually a crisis?
- Is the criticism valid?
- What's the damage scale?

### Step 3: RESPOND (within 4 hours)

**Response options:**
- **Option A:** Apologize + fix (if you did wrong)
- **Option B:** Clarify + context (if misunderstanding)
- **Option C:** Defend + stand firm (if you're right)
- **Option D:** Ignore + wait (if single person)

### Step 4: AMPLIFY
- Pin response to comments
- Post to Stories
- Post Reel addressing it
- Email followers
- Update bio if major

### Step 5: DOCUMENT
- Screenshot everything
- Comments
- DMs
- Your response
- Updates

---

## 3. RESPONSE TEMPLATES

**Template 1: Apologize + Fix**
```
"I acknowledge [issue] and apologize.

You were right. Here's what I got wrong [specific]:

Here's what I'm doing [action + timeline]:

I appreciate the feedback."
```

**Template 2: Clarify + Context**
```
"This could be misunderstood. Let me clarify:

[Original] meant [actual meaning], not [what thought].

Here's the full context [explain]:

Thanks for giving me chance to explain."
```

**Template 3: Stand Firm**
```
"I appreciate your perspective. My stance:

[Your position]

I understand you disagree, okay. But I'm standing by this:

[Reasoning]"
```

**Template 4: Private Resolution**
```
"I see concern. I'd like to resolve PRIVATELY.

Can you DM me?

[Contact info]"
```

---

## 4. HACKS VALIDATED

**HACK #1: Respond with video** (+300%)
- Not text
- Video where you speak directly
- Shows emotion + sincerity
- Much more powerful

**HACK #2: Tag them in response** (+200%)
- Response feels more personal
- They see it immediately
- Shows you're addressing them

**HACK #3: Call out the good** (+150%)
- "I appreciate you holding me accountable"
- "Thank you for feedback"
- "This helps me be better"
- Turns critic into ally

**HACK #4: Show change immediately** (+100%)
- Don't just apologize
- Actually change something
- Within days show proof
- People see follow-through

**HACK #5: Ask community** (+80%)
- "Help me understand"
- "What would be better?"
- Community feels heard
- Becomes collaborative

---

## 5. DO's AND DON'Ts

✅ DO:
- Respond fast
- Be sincere
- Acknowledge valid points
- Take responsibility
- Show action
- Thank people

❌ DON'T:
- React emotionally
- Blame others
- Delete everything
- Go silent
- Make excuses
- Mock critic
- Threaten followers

---

## 6. MONITORING DURING CRISIS

Check every 2 hours for first 24 hours:
- [ ] How many new comments?
- [ ] Sentiment improving/worse?
- [ ] Spreading to new platforms?
- [ ] Journalists contacting?
- [ ] Coordinated effort?
- [ ] Followers defending you?

Adjust response if needed:
- If worse = escalate
- If stable = maintain
- If improving = add gratitude

---

## 7. WHEN TO ESCALATE

Escalate to lawyer when:
- [ ] Defamation (false statements)
- [ ] Death threats
- [ ] Legal action threatened
- [ ] Contract dispute
- [ ] Harassment campaign

Your response: "Consulting legal team. Will address when appropriate."
