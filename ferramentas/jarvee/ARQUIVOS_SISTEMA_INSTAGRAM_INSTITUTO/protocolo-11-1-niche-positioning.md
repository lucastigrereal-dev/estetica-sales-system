## 2. NICHE FORMULA

**Niche = [Specific audience] + [Specific problem] + [Unique approach]**

### AUDIENCE (Who?):
- Solopreneurs
- Small business owners (2-50)
- Moms
- Fitness enthusiasts
- College students
- Coaches/consultants
- **Key:** Specific demographic

### PROBLEM (What pain?):
- Can't get clients
- Stuck at growth plateau
- Burned out
- No systems
- Don't know pricing
- Imposter syndrome
- **Key:** Specific pain point

### UNIQUE APPROACH (How?):
- Your method (organic only vs ads)
- Your philosophy (sustainable vs hustle)
- Your experience (went from broke to $100k)
- Your style (no BS, straight talk)
- **Key:** What makes you different

---

## 3. DISCOVERY PROCESS

**Step 1: Audit history (30 min)**
- What problems solved personally?
- What people ask constantly?
- Where do you have unfair advantage?
- What are you obsessed about?
- Output: 5-10 potential niches

**Step 2: Validate demand (1 hour)**
- Google search volume
- Reddit discussions
- Twitter sentiment
- FB Groups existence
- YouTube video count

**Step 3: Evaluate competition (1 hour)**
- Instagram creators in niche
- Follower ranges
- Engagement quality
- Saturation level
- Market gaps

**Step 4: Choose niche (30 min)**
- Passionate about? (2+ years)
- Proven demand? (evidence)
- Reasonable competition? (<1000 OR gaps)
- Premium pricing? ($$)
- Personal authority? (can speak it)

---

## 4. POSITIONING STATEMENT TEMPLATE

```
I help [SPECIFIC AUDIENCE]
who struggle with [SPECIFIC PROBLEM]
to [SPECIFIC OUTCOME]
using my proprietary [UNIQUE METHOD]
so that [TRANSFORMATION RESULT]
```

**Example:**
```
I help solopreneurs who struggle to attract high-ticket clients
to land premium clients consistently
using my LinkedIn Visibility Method
so they can scale to 6-figures without ads or agencies
```
